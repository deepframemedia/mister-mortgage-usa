Who Qualifies?

  • First-time buyer (no ownership in last 3 years)

  • Completion of homebuyer education course (some programs)

FAQ

Common Questions

What is a first-time homebuyer exactly?+
Most programs define a first-time homebuyer as someone who hasn't owned a primary residence in the past three years. This means if you owned a home years ago but have been renting, you may still qualify.
What first-time buyer programs are available in Florida?+
Florida offers several programs through the Florida Housing Finance Corporation, including the Florida Homeownership Loan Program (FL HLP), Florida Assist ($10,000 deferred loan), and county-specific programs with grants of $5,000-$25,000.
How long does it take to get pre-approved?+
At Mister Mortgage USA, we can typically issue a pre-qualification letter within 24 hours. A full pre-approval with verified documents usually takes 2-3 business days.
