FHA Loans Make Homeownership Possible

The Federal Housing Administration (FHA) loan program was designed to make homeownership accessible to Americans who might not qualify for conventional financing. With a down payment as low as 3.5% and flexible credit requirements, FHA loans open the door to homeownership for millions of buyers each year.

Mister Mortgage USA is an FHA-approved lender with deep expertise in South Florida's market. We guide first-time buyers and those with less-than-perfect credit through the entire FHA process, from pre-qualification to closing.

Who Qualifies?

  • Credit score 580+ for 3.5% down; 500-579 for 10% down
  • Steady employment history (2+ years preferred)
  • Property must be your primary residence
  • Property must meet FHA minimum standards
  • Mortgage Insurance Premium (MIP) required
FAQ

Common Questions

What is the FHA loan limit in Miami-Dade County?+
FHA loan limits are updated annually. In Miami-Dade County, limits are among the highest in Florida due to the area's home prices. Contact us for the current year's specific limits.
Do I have to pay mortgage insurance with an FHA loan?+
Yes, FHA loans require both an upfront Mortgage Insurance Premium (UFMIP) of 1.75% of the loan amount and an annual MIP paid monthly. This protects the lender but does add to your monthly cost.
Can I refinance out of an FHA loan later?+
Absolutely. Once you've built sufficient equity (typically 20%), you can refinance into a conventional loan to eliminate the mortgage insurance premium and potentially get a better rate.
