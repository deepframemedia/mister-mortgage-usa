Federal Law Disclosures

Our mortgage services are subject to the following federal laws and regulations:

  • Truth in Lending Act (TILA): We provide clear disclosure of loan terms, annual percentage rates (APR), and total costs before you enter into a loan agreement.
  • Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA): We provide a Loan Estimate within 3 business days of receiving your application.
  • Fair Housing Act: We are prohibited from discriminating in any aspect of a mortgage transaction.
  • Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA): We collect and report data on mortgage applications as required by federal law.
  • Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act: We protect the privacy of your nonpublic personal information.
